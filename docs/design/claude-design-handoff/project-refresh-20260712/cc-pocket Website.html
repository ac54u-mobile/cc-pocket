How it works

原理

Your phone talks to your computer.
The relay only sees ciphertext.

手机直连你的电脑，
中继只看得到密文。

The relay only forwards opaque encrypted frames. Plaintext never leaves your phone and your computer.

中继只转发不透明的加密帧。明文永远不离开你的手机和你的电脑。
